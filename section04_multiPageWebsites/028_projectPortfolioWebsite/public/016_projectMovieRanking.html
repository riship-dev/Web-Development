The Best Movies According to Rishi

My top 3 movies of all time

Midsommar

"Midsommar" is a disturbing folk horror film that follows a group of friends as they become entangled in a sinister pagan ritual during a summer festival in Sweden.

Kill Bill

"Kill Bill" is a stylized revenge epic where a former assassin seeks vengeance against her former allies after being left for dead on her wedding day.

Django: Unchained

"Django Unchained" is a Quentin Tarantino film that tells the story of a freed slave who partners with a bounty hunter to rescue his wife from a brutal plantation owner.
